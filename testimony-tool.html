How to read this: The speaker says H. Your assessment of their sincerity (are they reporting what they believe?) and correctness (is their belief right?) together determine c(speaks truly), which in turn determines the evidential force of their testimony. The eikosogram shows the Bayesian update: the width of the H column is your prior, and the heights show how likely "says H" is in each column.
