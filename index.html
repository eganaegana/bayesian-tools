Scientific Communities

  • Division of Cognitive Labor
    Explore the relationship between problem-solving methods and theory choice in scientific communities
  • Zollman Effect Interactive Tool
    Explore how network structure affects the spread of knowledge in scientific communities
  • Selective-Sharing Propagandist Model
    Simulate how selective sharing of evidence by a propagandist can distort community beliefs (after Weatherall & O'Connor)
  • Biased-Production Propagandist Model
    Simulate how a propagandist running their own experiments and selectively publishing results can influence policymakers (after Weatherall & O'Connor)
  • Journalists as Unwitting Propagandists Model
    Explore how journalistic practices like “fair” balanced reporting can slow policymakers from reaching accurate beliefs (after Weatherall & O'Connor)
